About us Who is PIXY?
We are a little, selfmanaged organisation with the goal to extend and promote the active schedule of the lovely Kpop Girlgroup PIXY PIXY is a 5-member Kpop Girlgroup under Allart Entertainment. They debuted 2021 with their song Wings. The group consists of Dia, Lola, Sua, Dajeong and Rinji.



Financial problems More fans... ...More PIXY
Due to financial problems, Allart Entertainment is unable to promote PIXY enough to gain any fans We are here to make sure that PIXY gets the love and attention they deserve through concerts, schedules and SNS Together we can assure that the girls can continue to live their dream. The more we are the better. So please help us to make PIXY a better known group.
